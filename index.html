Your weekly structure

Built for bigger glutes and overall tone using two 15 lb dumbbells, bodyweight, and condo-friendly movements. No jumping. Most workouts are 30–40 minutes.

Progression rules

  • Weeks 1–2: learn form, move slow, stop 2 reps before failure.
  • Weeks 3–4: add 1 set to the first two strength exercises.
  • Weeks 5–6: use slower lowering: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause.
  • Weeks 7–8: push close to failure on the final set while keeping perfect form.

Diet support

For bigger glutes without sloppy weight gain, eat like you train: consistent protein, steady calories, and enough carbs to perform.

Protein target100–130g/day

Simple meal template

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt or protein oats with fruit and peanut butter.
  • Lunch: chicken wrap or rice bowl with vegetables and a high-protein sauce.
  • Dinner: lean protein + carb + vegetables. Example: chicken, rice, green beans.
  • Snack: protein shake, cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, eggs, or tuna wrap.

Results rule

If your weight drops and glutes look flatter, eat more. If waist jumps quickly, reduce snacks or sauces. Aim for slow changes, not chaos.
